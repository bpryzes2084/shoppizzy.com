Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about shopping and selling on Shoppizzy.

Orders & Shipping
How do I place an order?
Browse products, click through to the product listing, and complete your purchase directly with the seller or retailer. Shoppizzy connects you with sellers who ship straight to your door — no middleman holding your order.
Who ships my order?
Each seller ships their own orders directly to you. Shoppizzy does not warehouse or fulfill products. Shipping times and carriers vary by seller and are listed on each product page.
How long does shipping take?
Shipping times depend on the individual seller and your location. Each listing includes estimated delivery times. If you have questions about a specific order, contact the seller directly.
Can I track my order?
Yes. Once your order ships, the seller will provide tracking information. Check your email for shipping confirmation or contact the seller if you haven't received tracking details.
Returns & Refunds
What is the return policy?
Return policies are set by each individual seller and vary by listing. Check the product page for the seller's return policy before purchasing. If you have an issue with your order, reach out to the seller first.
What if I receive a damaged or wrong item?
Contact the seller directly as soon as possible with photos of the item. Most sellers will arrange a replacement or refund. If you're unable to resolve the issue with the seller, contact us at support@shoppizzy.com.
Payments
What payment methods are accepted?
Accepted payment methods depend on the seller. Most sellers accept major credit and debit cards. Some listings may also support PayPal. Payment options are shown at checkout.
Is my payment information secure?
Yes. Payments are processed through secure, encrypted payment gateways. Shoppizzy does not store your credit card information.

Getting Started
How do I become a seller on Shoppizzy?
Register for a seller account at shoppizzy.com/seller/register. Fill in your shop details, contact information, and PayPal email for payouts. Your application will be reviewed and you'll hear back within 1–2 business days.
Is there a fee to sign up or list products?
No. Signing up and listing products on Shoppizzy is completely free. There are no monthly fees, no listing fees, and no storage fees — ever. List unlimited products and pay nothing until you make a sale.
Does Shoppizzy store or warehouse my inventory?
No. Unlike Amazon FBA, Shoppizzy does not hold or warehouse your inventory. You store your own products and ship them directly to buyers when an order comes in. This means zero storage fees and full control over your stock.
Fees & Payouts
Is there a limit on how many products I can list?
No limit — ever. List as many products as you want for free. Whether you have 1 item or 10,000, Shoppizzy lets you list them all at no cost. You only pay when you make a sale.
How does Shoppizzy make money?
Shoppizzy charges a simple 10% commission on each completed sale. There are no hidden fees, no subscription charges, and no storage costs. You keep 90% of every sale.
Are there any storage fees?
No. Shoppizzy never charges storage fees. You hold your own inventory, so there is nothing to store on our end.
How and when do I get paid?
Payouts are sent to your PayPal account. You'll need to provide your PayPal email when registering. Payout schedules and details are confirmed during the onboarding process.
What is the full breakdown of seller fees?
It's simple:

• Listing fee: $0
• Monthly fee: $0
• Storage fee: $0
• Commission per sale: 10%

That's it.
Orders & Fulfillment
How do I fulfill an order?
When a customer places an order, you ship it directly to them from your location. You choose your own carriers and packaging. You are responsible for timely fulfillment and providing tracking information to the buyer.
Who handles returns?
You set your own return policy and handle returns directly with buyers. Make sure your return policy is clearly stated on your product listings to avoid disputes.
Can I sell in any product category?
Most product categories are welcome. Prohibited items include counterfeit goods, hazardous materials, and anything that violates applicable laws. If you're unsure whether your products are eligible, contact us before listing.
