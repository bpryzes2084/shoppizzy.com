Returning a GrowceryWorld or Direct Product

Products that ship directly from GrowceryWorld or our Square catalog are fulfilled by Shoppizzy and are eligible for our 30-day return policy.

  1. Contact Us Email hello@shoppizzy.com with your order number and reason for return. We'll respond within 1–2 business days with a return authorization.
  2. Pack Your Item Repack the item securely in its original packaging. Include a note with your order number inside the box.
  3. Ship It Back Ship to the address provided in your return authorization. We recommend using a tracked shipping service. Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer unless the item was damaged or incorrect.
  4. Receive Your Refund Once we receive and inspect the item, your refund will be processed within 5–7 business days to your original payment method.

Returning an Affiliate Product

When you click a product link on Shoppizzy and purchase from a merchant website such as FragranceShop, Newegg, Famous Footwear, or any other partner, that transaction is between you and the merchant directly.

Returns for these purchases must be initiated through the merchant's own return process. Shoppizzy is not able to process refunds for purchases made on third-party merchant websites.

If you need help locating a merchant's return policy, contact us and we'll do our best to point you in the right direction.

Damaged or Incorrect Items

If your order arrives damaged or you received the wrong item, contact us within 14 days of delivery at hello@shoppizzy.com. Please include:

✦  Your order number

✦  A clear photo of the damaged item or incorrect product

✦  A brief description of the issue

We will arrange a full refund or replacement at no cost to you, including return shipping.
